Who is Tobë?

Born in Nigeria in 1999, Tobë's family moved to the United States before he could even read. He started with music at an early age, beginning with singing at school and playing piano at home, and eventually joined his middle school band. It wasn't until 2017, however, that he released his first independent EP, "Game of Love."


After a long hiatus spent learning music production and audio engineering, Tobë returned in late 2019 with his second EP, "Synths, Sounds, and Sweet Serenity."


Tobë plays 5 instruments, sings, and produces his own music from start to finish. He cites Stevie Wonder, Prince, Michael Jackson, and Anderson .Paak as his biggest musical influences.
